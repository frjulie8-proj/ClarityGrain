How we work

No jargon.

Our philosophy

Small food businesses don't need enterprise analytics — they need one clear answer to one real problem. Every engagement starts with listening, not pitching. We figure out where the pain is, find the data that speaks to it, and hand you something actionable.

01
Day 1 — free

We look at your reviews

Before we ask for anything, we analyze your public Yelp and Google reviews and send you a one-page summary of what we found. No login access, no contract, no cost.

34% of your 1-star reviews mention wait times — all clustering between 8–9am on weekdays.
02
Week 1 — discovery

We listen before we build

A 30-minute conversation in your space. We ask about your POS system, your busiest hours, what's been worrying you. We don't pitch. We take notes and figure out where data can actually help.

Tell me about the days when things feel off. What does a bad Tuesday look like for you?
03
Week 1–2 — analysis

We connect your data

With your permission we pull your POS data and clean it. We build a simple dashboard and run the analysis. You don't need to do anything technical.

You're ordering 40 croissants every Monday. You're selling 18 on average. That's $220 in waste every single week.
04
Week 2 — delivery

One clear recommendation

A dashboard link you can open on your phone and a one-page summary with one specific action. Not ten suggestions — one. The thing most likely to save you money in the next 30 days.

Order 22 croissants on Mondays. Check back in a month and tell us how much you saved.
05
Month 2 onward — optional

We keep finding things

If the first insight saved you money, we can keep looking once a month for $150. If it didn't, you owe nothing beyond the original fee.

You saved $180 last month. What if I found you one more thing like that, every month, for $150?
